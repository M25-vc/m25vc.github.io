Role


Operations Assistant

M25 is seeking a driven, self-starter who can juggle busy calendars, marketing, event management and other operational activities. This role is required to wear many hats and no two days are the same. The ideal candidate for this job is resourceful, a good problem solver, detail-oriented, organized, and takes initiative. Professionalism is key to success in this position, as this role will not only be working with all levels of the organization internally, but will also manage external relationships, often being the first person to interact with guests and entrepreneurs.

Start date: Mar or Apr 2021

Responsibilities:
  • Administrative needs of senior team - scheduling, travel logistics, and communication support, other HR-related tasks on behalf of team
  • Manage vendors - managing, auditing, and paying vendors, e.g. office rent, medical insurance, electricity, internet, meals, snacks, phone, office cleaning, events
  • Support culture - plan events that contribute to a positive, desirable work culture. Team dinners, birthday celebrations, and willing to help with larger event planning, dinners, and events that M25 organizes such as our Summit
  • Special projects – there are always one-off or yearly projects that we need to tackle i.e. ordering SWAG for the team and portfolio, Holiday Gifts for our investors, thank you gift for someone in our network for sourcing us a deal, etc.
Qualifications:
  • Prior experience in a support role preferred
  • Demonstrates excellent communication skills, written and verbal
  • Highly organized strong attention to detail and thoroughness of work product
  • Strong work ethic, enthusiastic, enjoys being part of a high performing team
  • Ability to take initiative, improvise to solve problems and always think proactively
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, ability to adapt to different personalities and work styles
  • Bachelor’s degree preferred
Compensation:
  • The salary for this role is competitive and commensurate with experience.
  • M25 also offers a benefits package!

We are committed to building a culture of inclusion, entrepreneurship, and innovation across gender, race, age, sexual orientation, religion, disability, and identity.
