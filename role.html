Role


Talent Intern (Summer 2022)

M25, an early stage venture capital firm with offices in Indianapolis and Chicago, seeks a talent intern to work closely with our platform team this summer. As part of the platform team, this person will directly support our portfolio of 125 companies by increasing our firm’s pipeline of jobseekers and to generate a quicker turnaround time from job posted to job filled.

WUnlike traditional roles at venture capital firms, this is not an investment-related internship and does not require knowledge of venture capital, startups, or finance. As the competition for talent at tech startups increases, we’re looking for someone interested in building M25’s talent pipeline and processes with us. Because colleges and universities don’t typically offer majors in Talent, we welcome applicants from any area of study interested in helping build out a new program within our organization.

Roles and responsibilities:
  • Researching meetup groups, forums, and digital communities for specific skills/roles common at tech companies
  • Generating contact lists of potential jobseekers
  • Designing and sending email campaigns to potential jobseekers
  • Using our existing talent network and jobs board to help connect our portfolio companies with curated talent
  • Working with our platform team on new strategies for recruiting talent
Skills:
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills: in addition to working with our team, you’ll also be communicating with CEOs and hiring managers at our companies as well as prospective job candidates
  • Highly collaborative and team-oriented: this role is part of the greater platform team and will involve working together on projects
  • Basic knowledge of Google Sheets

This paid internship is anticipated to be 10-15 hours per week with the option to expand hours if the role grows. If desired, we can work with you for internship credit through your program as well.

This internship can be done remotely; however, if you’re based in Indianapolis, IN or the surrounding area, you’re welcome to work in the office with the team as your schedule allows.

Our Commitment to Inclusion:

We are keenly aware that diverse teams make better decisions, and we welcome candidates of every race, religion, sex, gender, national origin, citizenship, ethnicity, marital status, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity and gender expression, and veteran status.
