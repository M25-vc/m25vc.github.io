Role


Platform Internship

M25, the most active Pre-Seed and Seed stage venture capital firm investing in Midwest-based tech startups, is seeking a Spring and Summer intern to build on existing activities in marketing, branding, startup support, and other platform activities.

The M25 team is seeking someone interested in learning more about venture capital, startups, and why the Midwest is an amazing place to fund and grow companies. As part of this internship, the selected person will participate in some or all of the following activities:

  • Sit in on calls with startups in M25’s portfolio
  • Work with M25’s Head of Platform to develop the Club M25 Summit, our signature annual event
  • Engage with community champions throughout the Midwest to help grow our Midwest Startups brand and content for the site
  • Communicate with our network of other VC firms for the Club M25 Summit
  • Enhance M25’s digital and social media marketing
Preferred Skills and Interests:
  • Knowledge of social media platforms and strategies
  • Excellent written and verbal communication
  • Interest in learning more about startups and venture capital
  • Enthusiasm for the Midwest

We’re a highly collaborative firm and are looking for someone who is driven by curiosity and teamwork. This role will build upon existing frameworks and will bring new ideas to our initiatives.

The internship is expected to begin in April and wrap up in mid-September following our annual summit. To apply for this position, please send your resume and a brief intro paragraph explaining why you are interested in this role to katie [at] m25vc [dot] com no later than March 21st.

For more on M25: https://m25vc.com/

For more on Midwest Startups: https://midweststartups.com/

About M25

M25 is the most active pre-seed / seed stage venture capital firm in the Midwest. M25 has invested in 100+ startups since 2015. We invest in an array of companies across industries and business models throughout the region. The firm is collaborative, transparent, and celebrates the success of teammates. We’re a startup ourselves and embrace diverse perspectives!
