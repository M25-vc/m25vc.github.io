Role


Student Analyst

M25 is hiring a student analyst to assist with projects and day-to-day operations. This person should be interested in venture capital and willing to put in the time and effort for a wide array of tasks that will allow the individual to learn quickly how a VC firm functions.

Start date: Now or summer 2020

What you will be doing:
  • 30% Project-based work
    • Coding based projects to optimize workflow
    • Data analysis
    • Implementing marketing projects
  • 25% Investment research and analysis
    • Compiling due diligence & investment recommendations
  • 20% Administrative work
    • Event planning and organization
    • Social media
  • 15% Deal Flow work
  • 10% Education
    • Attending and participating in events, pitches, and internal meetings
    • Continuously staying up-to-date on tech and VC news
    • Networking within the Chicago/Midwest tech ecosystem
Requirements:
  • Coding experience
  • Initiative-taker
  • Hustler
  • Flexibility
  • Resourcefulness
  • Strong desire to work in VC
  • Strong analytical ability
To Apply:

Submit your resume and a cover letter describing

  1. Why you want to work in VC &
  2. Any previous coding projects that you worked on.
