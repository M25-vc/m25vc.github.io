Role

Chief of Staff

M25 Group We are looking for an individual who is efficient and comfortable being a member of a team. The ability to multi-task, while maintaining complex schedules and managing administrative support, is essential in this position. The ideal candidate for this job is resourceful, a good problem solver, organized, and takes initiative. Professionalism is key to success in this position, as this role will not only be working with all levels of the organization internally, but will also manage external relationships, often being the first person to greet guests and entrepreneurs.

Responsibilities

  • HR including healthcare, payroll, PTO, etc.
  • Paperwork, forms, reporting, bill payment
  • Marketing tasks - tweeting, posting, sharing; blog / content scheduling and management
  • Coordinating public relations communications; working to promote M25 / partners
  • Event organization & support
  • Travel planning
  • Scheduling / calendar management
  • Support hiring processes and intern programs
  • Support operational aspects of office such as evaluating new products for office use, and all logistical aspects of supplies and inventory
  • Various small, ad hoc tasks

Requirements/Qualifications

  • Strong writing and communication skills - ability to effectively communicate with all levels of the internal organization and with external partners
  • Excellent time management and organization skills
  • Strong leadership and management skills
  • Experience with marketing / social media
  • Experience managing reporting and presenting to management
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, frequently changing, start-up environment
  • College degree not mandatory, but helpful
  • Previous experience not required, but welcome

Brief Description of M25: M25 is one of the most active venture capital firms focused solely on early-stage investments in the Midwest. M25 has already invested in over sixty companies since their inception in 2015, and continues to invest in over twenty companies each year. Their collaborative, forward-thinking approach and diverse array of investments across industries and business models throughout the region has quickly established them as a key node in the Midwest startup ecosystem.
