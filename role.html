Role


Full-time Analyst

M25 is hiring an analyst to work on the investment team. This role is designed to provide the prospective candidate with exposure to all aspects of investing in Early & Seed stage companies. The candidate will be actively working with the entire investment team during due diligence and new initiatives for the fund.

If you are looking to be challenged every day, make a huge impact, then this job is for you. Ideal candidates are passionate about the VC industry and are willing to put in the time and effort in a wide array of tasks. This position requires (<20%) travel.

Start date: Summer or Fall 2019

What you will be doing:
  • 30% Deal-based work
    • Conduct due diligence
    • Compile investment recommendations
    • Conduct initial analyses
  • 20% Project-based work
    • Work with our portfolio companies to gather metrics and track progress
    • Implement marketing projects
    • Manage websites
  • 20% Education
    • Attend and participate in events, pitches, and internal meetings
    • Continuously stay up-to-date on tech and VC news
    • Network within the Chicago/Midwest tech ecosystem
  • 20% Data management & analytics
Requirements:
  • Strong desire to work in the VC industry
  • Strong analytical ability
  • Attention to detail
  • Initiative-taker
  • Hustler
Preferred:
  • Coding experience
  • Startup experience
To Apply:

Submit your resume and a cover letter describing

  1. Why you want to work in VC &
  2. One of your biggest hacks

About M25

The collaborative, forward-thinking approach and diverse array of investments across industries and business models throughout the region has quickly established our company as a nexus in the Midwest startup ecosystem.
