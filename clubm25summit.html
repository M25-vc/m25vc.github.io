Club M25 Summit 4.0


March 05 & 06


The Club M25 Summit is a 2-day event hosted twice a year with the goal of lifting portfolio companies, feeding that community, and creating better connectivity in the Midwest ecosystem abroad.

Aside from hundreds of 1-on-1 meetings between founders and investors, the summit consists of major activities for both investors and M25 backed companes.

Registration is now open for the Club M25 Summit 4.0 taking place March 05 & 06!!


Some key dates:


January 31 - Matchmaking form is sent out

February 14 - Deadline to submit matchmaking form

February 18 - Draft schedule is sent out to individuals

February 26 - Final schedule & calendar invites

March 2 - Final agenda is sent out
