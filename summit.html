About the Summit

The Club M25 Summit is an annual gathering of our portfolio companies and investors for a two-day event each fall. Designed to elevate founders, foster community, and enhance connectivity, the Summit facilitates thousands of one-on-one meetings alongside engaging activities breakout sessions, speakers and social dinners. Held every year the city of Chicago, this event offers an unparalleled opportunity for startups and investors alike to forge meaningful connections with leading VC firms from across the nation. As one of the largest gathering of investors in the Midwest each year, the Summit serves as a catalyst for innovation and growth, driving the region’s entrepreneurial landscape forward.

In addition to our flagship fall Summit, M25 hosts Match Week every Spring, offering a virtual iteration of the event. Match Week provides a flexible and asynchronous platform for portfolio companies and investors to connect at their convenience over the course of a week
